Who We Are

Descendants of the Cochoqua

The West Coast Cochoqua Tribe are the rightful First Peoples of the Langebaan Lagoon and Saldanha Bay coastline. Our ancestors, the Cochoqua — among the most powerful of the Khoikhoi nations — were the first to welcome ships into this bay and the first to defend their sovereign land. That spirit of dignity, resilience, and stewardship lives in us today.

Our community is guided by the values of ubuntu, environmental custodianship, and cultural revival. We work to reclaim and celebrate our language, traditions, and connection to the living coastal landscape that shaped our identity over millennia.

Through education, heritage programmes, and community development, the West Coast Cochoqua Tribe is building a future where our children can grow up proud of who they are — and where our coastal home is protected for the generations still to come.
